Meet Day — Your Pets & Home Deserve Someone Who Truly Cares

Hi! My name is Day. I am 23 years old and based in Amelia Island, Florida. I am passionate about animals and clean spaces and I have been caring for pets and homes since 2019.

I recently graduated with my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and I am currently in the process of earning my Master's degree in Cybersecurity. Building this business while pursuing my education has taught me the value of hard work, reliability, and showing up for the people who trust you.

My goal is to eventually hire background-checked, bonded, and insured contractors so that every animal gets the chance to be booked, loved, and cared for while their pet parents are away. For now every visit is personal and I treat every home and every pet as if they were my own.

I also have three animals of my own, a mini labradoodle, a dilute calico rescue cat, and a tuxedo rescue cat, so I truly understand how much your pets mean to you!

🐾 Pet Services Policies and Expectations

Meet and Greet

A free meet and greet is required for all first-time clients before any booking is confirmed. This allows your pets to get comfortable with me and gives me the chance to learn their routines, preferences, and any special needs.

Photo Updates

You will receive photo and video updates every single visit. For overnight stays you will receive daily photo spam so you always know your babies are happy and well cared for.

Overnight Departure Day Pricing

  • Departure before 12:00 PM: No charge, completely free
  • Departure between 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM: $65
  • Departure between 5:00 PM and 7:00 PM: $65
  • Departure at 8:00 PM or later: $95 (full overnight rate)

Access and Entry

Please provide entry instructions prior to your booking, such as a door code, key placement, or garage access. All information shared is kept completely confidential.

Medication Administration

We are happy to administer oral medications and supplements as part of your pet's regular care routine. Please provide clear instructions and all necessary supplies.

Emergency Protocol

In the event of a medical emergency your pet will be taken to the nearest veterinary clinic immediately and you will be contacted right away. Please provide your vet's information at the time of booking.

🏡 Cleaning Policies and Expectations

Come Home to a Clean Home

That is the goal. You leave for work, we handle everything, and you come home to a spotless, fresh space. No contact needed, no awkward walkthroughs, just results.

How to Get a Quote

Since this is a contactless service please send over concern area photos through our chat widget before booking. We will review them and provide a quote within a few hours. No in-person consultation needed!

Before Your Cleaning

  • Confirm your address and send entry instructions beforehand
  • Provide a door code, key placement, or leave the door unlocked
  • Put all pets in a safe space (although we are very pet friendly!)
  • Leave any sheets, laundry, or bedding you would like washed, folded, or changed in a visible spot
  • Send concern area photos through the chat prior to your appointment

Payment

Payment is due upon completion of services. You will receive an invoice once your cleaning is finished. We accept Cash App, Venmo, Zelle, and cash.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 48 or more hours in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 to 48 hours are subject to a 50% fee. Same-day cancellations are non-refundable.
